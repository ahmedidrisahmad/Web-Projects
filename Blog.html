Haramaya University which was founded in 1954 in Ethiopia. This university is accredited by Ministry of Education, Ethiopia; they offer a wide variety of courses & faculties/campus including College of Agriculture and Environmental Sciences; College of Medical Sciences; College of Educational and Behavioral Sciences; College of Business and Economics; College of Law; College of Veterinary Medicine; Institute of Technology; College of Natural and Computational Sciences; College of Computing and Informatics; School of Graduate Studies; College of Continuing and Distance Education; College of Social Science and Humanities; Institute of Pastoral and Agro-Pastoral Studies; College of Health Sciences.
